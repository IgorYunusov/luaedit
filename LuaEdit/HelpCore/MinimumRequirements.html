Minimum Requirements
Lua homepage


LuaEdit is trying to be compatible with as many Windows® platform as possible. Officially, LuaEdit is Win32 compatible only. That means it is compatible on any Windows® platform. However, the following requirements should more than meet the system requirements of LuaEdit. If you ever have trouble running LuaEdit on any Win32 platform you may visit the Contact Us section of our Official Web Site to report your problem. Here is the minimum requirement list:

  • 800MHz or faster
  • Windows 2000, XP
  • 64MB RAM or more
  • 30MB free hard disk space
