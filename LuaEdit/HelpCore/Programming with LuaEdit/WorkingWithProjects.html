Projects in LuaEdit can be very useful in many ways. They can group and manage files better than single units without projects. They also feature debug and revision informations.

The content of a project may consulted through the "Project Tree" window (Picture 1.1). In this window, single units and projects (including their member units) are displayed in a hierarchical view:

+ ---Single Units
¦
+ ---Project
     ¦
     + --- Units
Because more than one project at once can be opened, the idea of "Active Project" is used in synchronization with the "Project" menu (Picture 1.2).
