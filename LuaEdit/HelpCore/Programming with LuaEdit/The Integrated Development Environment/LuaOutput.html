The Lua Output Window
Topic Groups
Lua homepage


The "Lua Output" window is used to display any print or io.write statements made in your script. You can also double-click on one of the lines that is displayed and it will bring you to the line where the call to print or io.write was made. In a near future, this window will become more of a "virtual Lua console" where you will be able to enter commands that will be interpreted by the running Lua virtual machine.
